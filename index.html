Chris Pring

Chris is a potter who makes functional domestic stoneware and delicate porcelain pieces for the home as well as Goldsworthy-inspired garden sculptures in stoneware and terracotta. 

In 2012, he moved his pottery business from Dorset to Kellaton, a rural hamlet in deepest South Devon. His work has been exhibited in both Dorset and locally in South Devon and he is a member of the South Hams Arts Forum 'SHAF'.

Chris offers pottery lessons for both beginners and the more experienced and is always happy to take commissions.  His studio is open to visitors by appointment.
