Pottery lessons

The pottery will reopen on May 1st so if you’d like to book a lesson, send Chris an email.

Suitable for complete beginners or those with a little more experience who want to develop or brush up on their skills. Adults and children alike will find the experience enjoyable and rewarding.

An ideal Christmas or birthday present!  (Personalised gift vouchers available.)

Chris prefers to give individual tuition but will take two or more students by arrangement.

In a typical two-hour lesson you can expect to learn about preparing clay and simple hand-building techniques, but will spend most of your time 'throwing' pots on the wheel.

The cost of a two-hour lesson is £70 and includes all materials - clay, glazes and firing.  Many prefer a group of three two-hour lessons which would be £175.
